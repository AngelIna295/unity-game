# Laboratory Nightmare

A 3D survival horror game developed in Unity, inspired by the core gameplay mechanics of Five Nights at Freddy's while introducing exploration, resource management, and additional threats.

---

## About the Game

**Laboratory Nightmare** takes place in an active research facility where dangerous experimental creatures are being contained and studied.

The player works as a night security operator responsible for monitoring the laboratory, controlling security systems, and preventing hostile entities from entering the control room.

Throughout the night, various monsters roam the facility and attempt to reach the player. To survive, the player must monitor security cameras, manage electricity consumption, operate security doors, and deal with an unstable humanoid experiment located in a nearby room.

The main objective is simple:

**Survive for 20 minutes.**

---

## Gameplay Features

### Security Camera System

The laboratory is equipped with a network of surveillance cameras.

Players can:

- Monitor different areas of the facility.
- Track monster movements.
- Predict incoming threats.
- React before enemies reach the control room.

Using cameras consumes electricity, forcing players to balance information gathering and power conservation.

---

### Security Doors

The control room is protected by automated security doors.

Players can:

- Open and close doors manually.
- Block monster entry points.
- Defend themselves from incoming attacks.

However, keeping doors closed for too long increases power consumption.

---

### Power Management

Electricity is the most important resource in the game.

Power is consumed by:

- Security cameras
- Closed security doors
- Facility systems

Poor power management can leave the player vulnerable and unable to defend against approaching monsters.

---

### Humanoid Experiment

One of the laboratory's experiments is a humanoid creature kept under observation in a neighboring room.

The creature remains dormant most of the time, but it requires regular attention from the player.

If ignored for too long, it may awaken and cause a facility-wide disturbance.

Consequences include:

- Severe light flickering throughout the laboratory.
- Camera malfunctions.
- Increased electricity consumption.
- Reduced visibility and situational awareness.

These effects make it significantly harder to track monsters and manage resources.

---

## Win Condition

To successfully complete the game, the player must:

- Monitor all threats.
- Manage electricity efficiently.
- Prevent the humanoid experiment from causing disruptions.
- Survive for **20 minutes**.

---

## Lose Conditions

The player loses if:

- A monster enters the control room.
- A hostile creature successfully attacks the player.
- Security systems fail, making survival impossible.
